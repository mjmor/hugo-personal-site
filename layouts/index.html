Hello! My name is Max. I'm a student at the University of Michigan studying Computer Science and Data Science in the School of Engineering. I am currently a senior and my academic interests include computer networking, computer and network security, data systems, and machine learning. I am a member of Girls in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. I recently worked under Dr. Michalis Kallitsis researching new methods of anomaly detection for the future "smart" power grid. This past summer, I was a software engineering intern with Facebook. At Facebook, I worked on a distributed publisher-subscriber data streaming system known as Wormhole.

If you don't find me doing one of the aforementioned things, then you can probably find me buried in my computer customizing my desktop (linux hobbyist here), tinkering with a technology I'm unfamiliar with, working on a project, or catching up on the latest news.

Aside from programming and tech-stuff, I'm a musician, avid reader, and skateboarder. I play the guitar and drumset, and I love going to local shows to see my favorite bands perform live.
